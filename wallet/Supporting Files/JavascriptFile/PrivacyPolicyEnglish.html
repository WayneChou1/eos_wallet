Dear Users,

PPWallet development team (“we”) respects and protects the privacy of Users (“you” or “Users”). Our team will collect and use your Personal Information, generated from your use of PPWallet, in accordance with this Privacy Policy (“Policy”).

1.Information We Collect
1.1 Our wallet is without backstage service,the transfer operation is completed through the BP node.Price information comes from OTCBTC open API（https://github.com/otcbtc/otcbtc-exchange-api-doc）,the price will fluctuate,for reference only。
1.2 Your wallet password, private key, mnemonics, and Keystore on the PPWallet do not store or synchronize to the server concerned. We do not provide services to retrieve wallet passwords, private keys, mnemonics, and keystore.

2.How We Use Your Information
2.1 In the process of using PPWallet, we will only save your wallet password, private key, mnemonics, Keystore locally to ensure the normal use of the application. We will not collect and use your information in any way.

When you choose to refuse to provide relevant information, it means that you give up the related services using PPWallet.
3.2 Please note that PPWallet will access DApp in the future. You should carefully select and use third party DApp and properly protect your personal information. PPWallet is not responsible for the privacy protection of other third parties.

4.1 If you are a User outside of PRC, you shall fully understand and conform to the laws, regulations and rules in your jurisdictions which are relevant.
4.2 If you encounter any personal information usage problems while using the service, you can contact us by submitting feedback in PPWallet, etc.
4.3 You can view this policy and other service rules in PPWallet. We encourage you to consult the service agreement and privacy policy every time you use PPWallet.
